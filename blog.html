Important FAQ

What are the current donation campaigns?

We are fundraising for flood relief in Noakhali and Feni, Bangladesh, as well as for injured individuals in the Quota movement.

How can I track the impact of my donation?

We share updates on our website and social media about how your contributions are making a difference.

How will my donation be used?

Donations will provide essential supplies like food, clean water, and medical assistance to those affected by these crises.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Donations may be tax-deductible depending on local regulations. Check with your tax authority for details.
